## Strategic Risks to Watch

1. **Vanta is moving up-market** — they're adding more policy/process features. They have the distribution. Watch their roadmap.
2. **AI is commoditizing evidence collection** — Comp AI is already undercutting on price. Evidence collection alone is not defensible.
3. **Your real competition is spreadsheets and consultants** — most orgs your target size are still running compliance in Excel + Confluence. The pitch is replacing that, not replacing Vanta.
